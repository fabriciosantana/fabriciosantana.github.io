# Qualification

## Decision

`pursue discovery only`

## Summary

This is a strong fit with Fabricio Santana's positioning because it is not a simple app build. It is a social product with mobile clients, backend services, real-time messaging, moderation, authentication, notifications, analytics, and scalability concerns. However, the safest commercial move is to sell the Discovery Sprint first because the current scope is broad and the client's budget, decision process, product assets, and launch boundary are unknown.

## Qualification assessment

- Budget signal or likely ability to pay: unknown to moderate. The requested scope is broad enough that a serious client should expect a meaningful budget, but no budget range is stated.
- Clarity of scope: moderate. Core features are listed, but product rules, release strategy, monetization, moderation policy, and technical starting point are unknown.
- Fit with positioning: strong. This is a product, API, integration, security, and scalability opportunity.
- Urgency and business pain: likely real, but business deadline and launch strategy are not stated.
- Access to decision maker: unknown.
- Risk of uncontrolled scope: high. Dating apps often expand quickly into verification, abuse prevention, reporting, blocking, subscriptions, geolocation nuances, read receipts, and moderation workflows.
- Seriousness of client signals: moderate. The request is detailed and commercially meaningful, but platform, budget, and current assets are not stated.
- Likelihood of competing only on low price: medium. Dating-app requests often attract low-cost builders, so the proposal should differentiate on risk reduction and product architecture.

## Recommended commercial framing

Recommend selling the Discovery Sprint as the immediate next step. Use discovery to define the MVP boundary, confirm product assets, validate budget, choose the mobile/backend architecture, and decide whether the first implementation cycle should be `Product Acceleration` or `Scale & Intelligence`. The likely recommendation after discovery is `Scale & Intelligence` due to:

- mobile delivery for iOS and Android;
- real-time chat and push notifications;
- moderation and administrative tooling;
- authentication and social login;
- privacy and safety concerns;
- scalability and future growth requirements.

## Main sales angle

The proposal should make clear that the main challenge is not only building screens. The real work is designing a secure and scalable social product architecture, choosing the right MVP boundary, and controlling operational risks such as abuse, moderation, messaging reliability, notification delivery, and future product expansion. The message should avoid implying that a complete dating platform can be delivered in one four-week cycle.
