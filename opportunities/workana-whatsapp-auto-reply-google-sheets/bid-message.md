This is not only a WhatsApp bot task. The main risk is building a compliant and reliable support workflow where automatic replies, Google Sheets integration, routing rules, and human escalation work together without depending on unofficial or fragile automation.

I would start with a one-week Discovery Sprint to define the best WhatsApp-compliant architecture, confirm how Google Sheets should be used, map the FAQ and routing logic, and produce a practical implementation plan with acceptance criteria, provider recommendation, and validated scope.

From there, the first implementation can move into a fixed delivery cycle for the agreed automation workflow, usually Essential Delivery for a bounded FAQ/routing bot or Product Acceleration if the routing, monitoring, and operational handoff need more depth.
