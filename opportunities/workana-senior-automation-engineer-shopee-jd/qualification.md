# Qualification

## Decision

`pursue discovery only`

## Summary

This opportunity is technically strong but commercially ambiguous. The description starts as a Senior Automation Engineer role focused on QA automation for web, mobile, API, and CI/CD, but the final lines point to Shopee, JD, and "Powerful Scraping Tools", which suggests a different problem involving scraping infrastructure and third-party platform interaction. The safest move is to sell a Discovery Sprint first to clarify whether the real scope is test automation, scraping automation, or a hybrid.

## Qualification assessment

- Budget signal or likely ability to pay: unknown to moderate. The role sounds senior and specialized, but no budget range is provided.
- Clarity of scope: low to moderate. The request mixes QA automation language with scraping-tool language.
- Fit with positioning: strong on the technical side. Automation, backend integration, CI/CD, observability, and reliability are a good match.
- Urgency and business pain: likely real, but the operational objective is unclear.
- Access to decision maker: unknown.
- Risk of uncontrolled scope: high. The scope may expand into scraping resilience, data extraction pipelines, test architecture, mobile automation, API validation, and CI/CD hardening. Any scraping-related work should be limited to authorized data access, permitted APIs, compliant browser automation, and platform-policy boundaries.
- Seriousness of client signals: mixed. The request sounds senior and complex, but the problem statement is internally inconsistent.
- Likelihood of competing only on low price: medium. Scraping-related jobs often attract low-cost offers, but senior QA automation also attracts serious technical buyers.

## Recommended commercial framing

Recommend selling the Discovery Sprint as the immediate next step. Use discovery to determine:

- whether the main objective is QA automation, scraping automation, or both;
- whether Shopee and JD are test targets, scraping targets, or both;
- whether the client needs a delivery package or part-time embedded senior automation support;
- whether the first implementation cycle fits Product Acceleration or Scale \& Intelligence.

The likely package after discovery is:

- `Product Acceleration` if the confirmed scope is a bounded automation framework with test suites, CI/CD integration, and moderate platform complexity.
- `Scale & Intelligence` if the confirmed scope includes large-scale scraping workflows, resilience against platform changes, observability, security controls, or hybrid QA plus data-extraction infrastructure.

## Main sales angle

The proposal should make clear that the main challenge is not only writing automation scripts. The real work is defining the right automation architecture, separating quality automation from platform scraping, controlling reliability and maintenance risk, respecting platform-policy boundaries, and choosing an implementation model that matches the actual business objective: fixed delivery cycle or senior part-time support.
