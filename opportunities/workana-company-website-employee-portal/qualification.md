# Qualification

## Decision

`pursue discovery only`

## Summary

This opportunity is a strong fit because it combines website rebuild, brand implementation, bilingual content management, secure authenticated access, internal resource sharing, and maintainable delivery. The safest commercial move is to sell the Discovery Sprint first because the request combines two different systems with different risk profiles: a public company website and a secure employee portal.

## Qualification assessment

- Budget signal or likely ability to pay: moderate to strong. A full website recreation plus an authenticated portal suggests a meaningful budget.
- Clarity of scope: moderate. The top-level goals are clear, but content volume, portal features, existing stack, hosting model, authentication method, and CMS expectations are not.
- Fit with positioning: strong. This is a web product, internal tool, security, content management, and integration opportunity.
- Urgency and business pain: likely real. The client wants a brand refresh and an internal portal, which suggests both customer-facing and operational needs.
- Access to decision maker: unknown.
- Risk of uncontrolled scope: high. Authenticated portals often expand into roles, permissions, document management, announcements, notifications, audit logs, and future integrations.
- Seriousness of client signals: good. The request is concrete and business-oriented.
- Likelihood of competing only on low price: medium. Website rebuilds attract low-cost offers, but the employee portal and bilingual CMS needs create room for a more serious proposal.

## Recommended commercial framing

Recommend selling the Discovery Sprint as the immediate next step. Use discovery to confirm:

- the boundary between the public website and the employee portal;
- the authentication and authorization model;
- the content migration approach;
- the bilingual content strategy;
- the editing workflow and CMS needs;
- whether the first implementation cycle fits Product Acceleration or Scale \& Intelligence.

The likely package after discovery is:

- `Product Acceleration` if the scope is a well-bounded public website plus an internal portal with moderate security and content-management complexity.
- `Scale & Intelligence` if the portal requires stronger roles and permissions, document controls, auditability, integration with identity providers, or broader internal workflows.

## Main sales angle

The proposal should make clear that the main challenge is not only rebuilding pages with a new brand. The real work is separating public-site concerns from secure internal-portal concerns, preserving content and functionality, delivering bilingual editing workflows, and making the result maintainable for the client team after launch.
